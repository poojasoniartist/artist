About Me

I'm Pooja Soni, a passionate sketch artist dedicated to bringing human emotions and memories to life through graphite and charcoal pencil strokes. I specialize in highly detailed realistic portraits, caricature artwork, couples' sketches, and custom art commissions.

Every drawing is custom handcrafted, focusing on fine gradients, striking highlights, and textural details that capture the unique personality and true soul of the subject.
